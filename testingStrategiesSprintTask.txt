3. Boundary Value Analysis (BVA)
  3.1 Tasks
    - taskTitleLengthBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createTaskMutation.js; boundary: 0Vs1 and 200Vs201
    - taskDescriptionLengthBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createTaskMutation.js; boundary: 0Vs1 and 2000Vs2001
    - projectIdExistenceBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createTaskMutation.js; boundary: existingIdVsNonExistingId
    - assigneeIdExistenceBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createTaskMutation.js; boundary: existingUserVsNonExistingUser
    - taskStatusBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/changeTaskStatusMutation.js, graphql/mutations/createTaskMutation.js, graphql/mutations/updateTaskMutation.js; boundary: allowedStatusVsInvalid (e.g. "In Progress" vs "INVALID")
    - taskStatusMissingBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/changeTaskStatusMutation.js; boundary: nullVsPresent (expect 'Status is required')
  3.2 Sprints
    - sprintNumberBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createSprintMutation.js, graphql/mutations/updateSprintMutation.js; boundary: 0Vs1, non-integer vs integer, duplicateNumberWithinSameProject
    - sprintStartEndDateBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createSprintMutation.js, graphql/mutations/updateSprintMutation.js; boundary: startBeforeEnd, equalDates, empty vs present
    - sprintDurationBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createSprintMutation.js; boundary: minimalDurationVsZero
    - sprintDateFormatBoundary: files: graphql/mutations/createSprintMutation.js, graphql/mutations/updateSprintMutation.js; boundary: valid ISO date vs invalid string
